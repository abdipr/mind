# FL Studio

My escape from code. Making lofi beats or experimenting with synths.

## Mixing Tips

- Always **sidechain** the kick to the 808 so the low end doesn't clash.
- Don't put too many plugins on the Master channel before mixing is done.
- EQ: cut first, boost later. Subtractive before additive.

## Favorite Plugins

- **Serum** — Versatile synth
- **Valhalla Supermassive** — Incredible free reverb
- **iZotope Vinyl** — Instant lo-fi effect, free
