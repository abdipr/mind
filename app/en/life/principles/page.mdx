# Life Principles

These aren't rigid rules.

More like things that, so far, have just made sense to me.

## 1. Consistency over perfection

I'm a big believer in small, steady progress. Better to keep moving than to wait for perfection and never start.

## 2. Mistakes are fine, just don't repeat them

I mess up a lot. But it only becomes a real problem if I keep making the same mistake over and over.

## 3. Focus on what you can control

So much is out of our hands. I'd rather spend my energy on the things I can actually change.
