# Mental Models

Mental models help us simplify a complex world. Here are a few I use often:

## Inversion

Instead of asking "How can I succeed?", flip it: "How can I **guarantee failure**?" — then avoid those things.

Charlie Munger said: *"Invert, always invert."*

## Margin of Safety

Always leave room for error. In code, in finance, in time management.
Never be 100% optimistic without a backup plan.

This is also why I always estimate project timelines at 2x my initial guess.

## Second-Order Thinking

Don't just think about direct consequences. Think about the consequences of consequences.
Example: Big discount → sales up → but margins destroyed → cashflow collapse.
