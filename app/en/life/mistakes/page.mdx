# Mistakes

The most impactful lessons usually come from messing up.

## Waiting to be "ready"

I used to put things off because I didn't feel ready.

The truth is, "ready" usually comes after you start.

## Caring too much about what people think

The more I worried about others' opinions, the slower I moved.

Now, I'm just focusing on my own progress.
